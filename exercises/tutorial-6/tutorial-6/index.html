    Tutorial 6 ─ Unsupervised learning¶

    Cluster distance¶

    • x_1 = [1,3,4,5,2]​
    • x_2 = [1,4,2,3,2] ​

    Manhattan distance: \Sigma_{i=1}^{d}|x_i - y_i| Euclidean distance: \sqrt{\Sigma_{i=1}^{d}(x_i-y_i)^2}

    So, the solution is |1-1|+|3-4|+|4-2|+|5-3|+|2-2|=5
